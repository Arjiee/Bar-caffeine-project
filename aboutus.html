About Us

Mission

Bar Caffeine started as a humble startup with the owner being an former Starbucks employee, Bar caffeine mission is to provide ‘A’ class quality yet affordable coffee, iced coffee and tea brews for people to enjoy!

Vision

Bar Caffeine as one of the leading brand of coffee shop in the country, while maintaining the affordability and quality of our products.

How we started

Nag simula kami noong malapit na matapos ang pandemic, nag gagala kami noon sa divisoria sa kahahanap ng ma bubuy and sell dahil nga walang trabaho ang mga tao so kami rin wala. Hanggang sa naisipan namin na mag franchise na naging daan para bumoo kami ng sarili naming negosyo at brand, galing starbucks ang misis ko noon, sya narin gumawa ng branding namin, kaya makasisigurado ka sa quality ng mga kape at iced coffee namin. Nagtuloy tuloy na itong negosyo namin at nagkaroon na ng franchise sa ibang lugar.

Minsan kelangan mo talagang simulan at subukan.

Stevenson Arroyo
